FAQ

What is a digital menu, and how does it work?
A digital menu is an online version of a restaurant's menu, accessible through a website, app, or QR code. Customers can view the menu, customize their orders, and even place them directly from their devices.
Can I access the digital menu without downloading an app?
Yes, the digital menus are accessible via QR codes or direct links, so you don’t need to download any app. Just scan the code or click the link to view the menu instantly.
Can I see detailed descriptions and images of dishes on the digital menu?
Yes, digital menus often include detailed descriptions, ingredients, and images of dishes to help you make the best choice.
What if the digital menu doesn’t load or has issues?
If the menu doesn’t load, check your internet connection or refresh the page. If the issue persists, inform the restaurant staff for assistance.
Can I view the digital menu in my preferred language?
The digital menus offer language options, allowing you to switch to your preferred language for easier navigation.
